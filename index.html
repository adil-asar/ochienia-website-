ABOUT US

Cocktail at Ochenta Cocktail Bar

The newest addition in the trendy Pietermaai district, with knowledgeable and experienced bartenders serving cartful cocktails made with local brands and using the freshest ingredients. Housed in a picturesque bright pink colored monument on the block with an elegantly designed interior to match, Ochenta Cocktail Bar has created a very pleasant cozy experience and will be teaching you the tricks of the trade. Making a good cocktail is all about the right amounts and the right ingredients.
